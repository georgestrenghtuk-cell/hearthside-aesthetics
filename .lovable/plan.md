

## VeluwsePellets.nl — Aesthetic Marketing Website

A warm, modern single-page website designed to attract customers and drive sales for VeluwsePellets.nl, a Dutch wood pellet supplier from the Veluwe region.

### Brand & Design Direction
- **Color palette**: Warm oranges (from the logo), deep browns, creamy whites — evoking warmth, fire, and natural wood
- **Typography**: Clean, modern sans-serif with warm personality
- **Imagery**: Gradient overlays, flame/warmth motifs, cozy atmosphere
- **Logo**: Embedded from the uploaded file

### Page Sections (Single-Page, Dutch Language)

1. **Hero Section** — Full-width warm gradient background with tagline like "Warmte uit de Veluwe" (Warmth from the Veluwe), a compelling subtitle about quality ENplus A1 pellets, and a prominent CTA button ("Bestel Nu" / "Bekijk Assortiment") linking to veluwsepellets.nl

2. **USP Bar** — Three key selling points in icon cards:
   - ENplus A1 gecertificeerd (certified quality)
   - Levering binnen 3 werkdagen (fast delivery)
   - Betalen na levering (pay after delivery)

3. **Product Showcase** — Featured products (Forge, Anvil, Piniera, Ten Damme, My Pellets, Pellet Heater) displayed in attractive cards with prices, linking to the shop

4. **Why Choose Us** — Benefits section: duurzaam (sustainable), hoge warmte-afgifte (high heat output), schone verbranding (clean burn), scherpe prijzen (competitive prices)

5. **Customer Reviews** — The 5 real Google reviews displayed in a beautiful testimonial carousel, with the 4.9/5 average rating and 91 reviews prominently shown

6. **About / Trust Section** — Brief story about VeluwsePellets, pickup locations (Heerde, Voorhout, Marum), and trust signals

7. **CTA / Contact Section** — Final call-to-action with link to the webshop and contact info

8. **Footer** — Links, business info, branding

### Key Features
- Fully responsive (mobile-first)
- Smooth scroll animations
- All text in Dutch
- External links to the actual webshop for purchasing
- Warm, inviting aesthetic that builds trust
